Phyzok Teaching Fellows


Period and Post: 3 to 6 months, Internship
Role Summary: The role will focus on delivering workshops to school students.
Essential Responsibilities:

  • Prime facilitator for delivering PhyzokTM Workshops to middle and secondary school students.
  • Contribute to design and development of experiments in PhyzokTM Lab.
  • Participate actively with the back-end team of content designers to edit and create new content.
  • Communicate proactively with students and academia.
Qualifications/ Requirements:
  • Young graduates from IIT, BITS and NITs preferred.
  • Ability to think on one's feet and out of the box.
  • Strong in higher secondary science.
  • Excellent communication, articulation, analytical and problem solving skills.
  • Quick learner and a great team player.
  • Excellent customer interaction and relationship building skills.

Graphic Designer


Period and Post: Full time and Creative Designer
Role Summary: The role will focus on the complete design and management of any graphic/creative entity required by PhyzokTM and Imprezzo
Essential Responsibilities:

  • Complete design and redesign of any graphic entity asociated with PhyzokTM and when demanded.
  • Participate actively with the content designers to create graphic content .
Qualifications/ Requirements:
  • Creativity and artistic skill.
  • Good sense of space and colour.
  • Quick learner and a great team player.
  • Proficiency in Adobe Photoshop, 3DS Max, Maya and Adobe After Effects.
  • Skilled at innovation and ideation.
  • Ability to juggle multiple tasks at the same time.

Web Developers and Coders


Period and Post: Full time and Software solution provider
Role Summary: The role will focus on the development and mangement of offline and online presence and indigenous softwares.
Essential Responsibility:

  • Work closely with team members to evaluate software products and develop new applications to support key strategic business priorities.
  • Responsible for coding, testing, and implementation of solutions within established standards
Qualifications/Requirements:
  • Strong coding skills.
  • Proficient in Java EE, PHP, Javascript, HTML, CSS.
  • Quick learner and a great team player.
  • Previous experience in any corpo.
  • Skilled at innovation and ideation.
  • Ability to juggle multiple tasks at the same time.
